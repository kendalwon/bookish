TIPS READS TOYS STORES

This book list for kids 13 and up includes a variety of genres: fantasy, realism, historical fiction and more. Every teen can find something that will get them excited about reading!

The Book Thief

Markus Zusak

An intense and enduring story of a girl in Nazi Germany.

Coraline

Neil Gaiman

Coraline is a delightfully funny, scary, fairy-tale novel.

Thieving Weasels

Billy Taylor

A funny page-turner that keeps you guessing.

The Graveyard Book

Neil Gaiman

A boy raised by ghosts faces the words of the living and the dead.

The Giver

Lois Lowry

A boy in a seemingly idea world discovers dark secrets.

The Last Mission

Harry Mazer

A 15-year-old lies his way into the Air Force during WWII.

The Hunger Games Trilogy

Suzanne Collins

These bestsellers are gripping, powerful, and unforgettable.

Percy Jackson and the Olympians Series

Rick Riordan

Thrilling adventures with Greek gods and monsters.

The Maze Runner Series

James Dashner

A dystopian science fiction novel about teens trying to escape from a maze.

The Fault in Our Stars

John Green

A popular romance between two terminally ill teens.

To Kill a Mockingbird

Harper Lee

One of the most cherished stories of all time, for good reason.

The Divergent Series

Veronica Roth

A dystopian tale that emphasizes the power of our choices.
