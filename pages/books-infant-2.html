TIPS READS TOYS STORES

Babies love books! From traditional favorites to modern classics, here are some fantastic books for the littlest of littles.

Dear Zoo

Rod Campbell

A simple lift-the-flap story with a surprise on every page.

Goodnight Moon

Margaret Wise Brown

Sweet, calming rhymes have made this a favorite for decades.

Little Blue Truck

Alice Schertle

A fun tale that'll have little ones laughing until the last page.

The Very Hungry Caterpillar

Eric Carle

Go along on a culinary adventure with this hungry arthropod.

Moo Baa La La La

Sandra Boynton

This rhyming book of silliness has inviting pictures and lots of funny noises to entertain!

Giraffes Can't Dance

Giles Andreae

This giraffe teaches little ones to follow their dreams.

Brown Bear, Brown Bear, What Do You See?

Bill Martin, Jr.

A classic collection of colorful animals.

On The Night You Were Born

Nancy Tillman

Adults and kids alike will love this sweet book.

Where is Baby's Belly Button?

Karen Katz

A fun lift-the-flap book to teach basic body parts.

Llama Llama Red Pajama

Anna Dewdney

This lovable llama learns to sleep on his own.

Good Night, Gorilla

Peggy Rathmann

This is a story you make up as you go. Perfect for little imaginations!

The Snowy Day

Ezra Jack Keats

Kids love this journey through the cold with little Peter.
