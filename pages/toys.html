TIPS READS STORES

Young children learn best through play. We have curated an up-to-date collection of toys and games that kids will love to learn from!

My First Banagrams

Perfect for the early learner, this game helps children grow a love for wordplay, and aligns with reading curriculums.

Roll A Story Dice

Help kids generate creative stories with these beautifully illustrated dice.

The Memory Palace

Kids of all ages can exercise their memory, imagination, and develop storytelling skills with no reading required.

Storytelling Finger Puppets

Encourage creative play for the whole family while building language and storytelling skills.

Zingo!

Perfect for pre-readers and early readers, builds language and matching skills in preschool kids through fun, fast-paced play.

Happy Hats

Children explore letter names and sounds as they create simple words to help pave the path to reading success.

Phonics Dominoes

Children will learn to build hundreds of words from this hands-on exploration of phonics.

Wooden Reading Blocks

These blocks help boost kids' confidence in letter sounds while enhancing their fine motor skills.

Adventures in Storyland!

Early learning story-telling game where kids use their imagination to create fantastic adventures. No reading required!

Play-Doh Letters

Set of colorful letter and number molds lets kids mix and match ABCs and 123s for endless spelling and counting fun Play-Doh-style.
