TIPS READS TOYS STORES

Early elementary students need books that meet their interests. The books we've selected can be read by newly independent readers or would work well as a family read-aloud.

Calvin and Hobbes

Bill Watterson

The antics of these two will keep kids laughing AND reading.

Elephant and Piggie Books

Mo Willems

Funny and easy to read, these stories are sure to be favorites.

Junie B. Jones Series

Barbara Park

These books about the sassy and hilarious Junie B. are page turners.

The Magic Treehouse Series

Mary Pope Osborne

Suspenseful and adventurous tales that take place all over the world.

Mercy Watson Books

Kate DiCamillo

Fantastic illustrations accompany these fun stories about a sweet pig.

Ramona Books

Beverly Cleary

There's no way to choose a favorite - every Ramona tale is well crafted and entertaining.

Matilda

Roald Dahl

Whimsical and magical, kids will feel like they are part of the story.

My Father's Dragon

Ruth Stiles Gannett

A young boy travels to an island to rescue a dragon in this classic novel.

Good Night Stories for Rebel Girls

Elena Favilli and Francesca Cavallo

100 stories about the life of 100 extraordinary women.

Charlotte's Web

E.B. White

This touching story has been a favorite for decades.

Pippi Longstocking

Astrid Lindgren

9-year-old Pippi is the quirky friend every kid wishes they had.

The Hundred Dresses

Eleanor Estes

A must-read for every child, this book teaches about acceptance and friendship.
