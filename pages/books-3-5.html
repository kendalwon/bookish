TIPS READS TOYS STORES

Preschoolers are ready to absorb stories, so make sure you have great ones on hand! These books will keep 3-5 year olds coming back for more.

The Giving Tree

Shel Silverstein

This story of selfless generosity warms every heart.

The Dark

Lemony Snicket

A fun adventure into the dark that helps little Laszlo overcome his fear.

Frog and Toad are Friends

Arnold Lobel

Short tales featuring these friends who learn and grow together.

King Bidgood's in the Bathtub

Don and Audrey Wood

Beautifully detailed illustrations help make this catchy story a favorite.

The Gruffalo

Julia Donaldson

The story of a clever mouse, told in an unforgettable rhythm.

Knuffle Bunny

Mo Willems

Every kid can relate to Trixie, who loses her beloved bunny in NYC.

The Paper Bag Princess

Robert Munsch

Heroine Elizabeth outsmarts a dragon to save the prince in this classic.

Where the Wild Things Are

Maurice Sendak

Unique illustrations highlight Max's imaginative adventure.

Stellaluna

Janell Cannon

Both informative and sentimental, this story will please every child.

Square

Mac Barnett

One of a trilogy, Square is a must-read for every preschooler.

The Snail and the Whale

Julia Donaldson

Almost musical in its flow, this story is both exciting and soothing.

Pete the Cat and the Missing Cupcakes

Kimberly and James Dean

A mystery featuring the well-known lovable feline.
